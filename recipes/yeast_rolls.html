Yeast Rolls

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup melted salted butter + 1tbsp reserved
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 2 packets active dry yeast
  • 1 cup of water warmed to 115F
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3 cups of all purpose flour (ideally sifted)

Steps

  1. Combine water, melted butter, sugar and yeast in a bowl. Mix until the yeast is broadly incorporated.
  2. Wait 10-15 minutes for the yeast to activate (the activated yeast will rise on top of the butter).
  3. Pour yeast mixture into a bowl and gradually incorporate the salt and flour.
  4. When the dough is workable (slightly tacky, but doesn't stick to your hands), knead for 6-8 minutes.
  5. Portion into roughly 10 rolls on a parchment-lined baking sheet.
  6. Cover and let rest in a warm place for 30 minutes (you can preheat the oven to 400F at this time).
  7. Baste the tops of each roll with reserved butter.
  8. Cook for 8-10 minutes at 400F or until golden.
